About Deep Sea Fish

Deep Sea Fish are fish that live deep underwater. They are considered by many, monsters, and can shapeshift into any form they choose - there could be one in the room you are in right now! We have been able to identify some shapeshifting deep sea creatures, such as Tristan.

Some deep sea fish are nice and friendly, others on the other hand, are annoying (exactly like humans.)

Deep sea fish choose their form to fit in with their surroundings, so it could disguise itself as a table, a lamp or even a human.
